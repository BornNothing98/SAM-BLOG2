THE MAYOR - Movie 2024

In a small town plagued by lawlessness, a group of bandits hatches a daring plan to assassinate their mayor, hoping to seize control and rule with impunity. However, their carefully orchestrated plot goes awry, and the mayor narrowly escapes the attempt on his life. Enraged by the betrayal and the threat to his authority, the mayor embarks on a relentless pursuit to bring the bandits to justice. With the support of loyal bodyguard and a newfound determination, he turns the tables on the criminals, using his cunning and resources to outmaneuver them at every turn.

Genre: Action
